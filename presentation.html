Values and Mission

  • Make sure everyone fully agrees with the company goals and values
  • Hire leadership that supports 100% your methods and represents you best
  • Poisonous people can kill your business

Build a Team Member Profile

Qualities of a Remote Soldier

  • Accountable
  • Team player
  • Self-driven, motivated
  • Learns fast and knows how to find solutions to problems
  • Takes ownership of a problem/product
  • Wears multiple hats
  • Skills are not the most important bit

21 Months In: How to Manage a Remote Team

Wade Foster, Zapier says:

  • Hire Doers
  • Hire people you can trust
  • Trust the people you hire
  • Hire people who can write
  • Hire people who are ok without a social workplace

Job Description, Interview, Hiring

  • Detailed and descriptive
  • Focus on communication, responsibility, logical thinking
  • Trial projects and trial milestones
  • Set expectations and measure everything
  • Always refer to the company goals and values
  • Only 2% hiring success rate at DevriX

Put Your Team To Work

Agile to the Resque

  • Monday Kick-off meetings
  • Weekly sprints and reviews
  • Keep track of each one's progress
  • Easy to direct and navigate in daily standups
  • Track availability and report immediately to clients

Tools are Paramount

  • Tools and services are your friends!
  • All sorts of automation, build scripts, standardization
  • Flow everything into HipChat - commits, tasks, etc
  • Work estimates -> Reports -> Reviews -> Invoices

Employee retention

An Ongoing and Repetitive Journey...

Procecise everything

  • DRY and KISS all the time
  • make it easier for new people (and yourself)
  • predictable and less disappointing when in trouble
  • let's you focus on growing your business

Communication, Communication, Communication

  • Always strive for crisp communication
  • Drastically improves PM and implementation planning
  • Prevents drama, delays and mistakes immediately
  • Strengthens the team spirit and vibe
  • Complete transparency and access to the C-level executives
